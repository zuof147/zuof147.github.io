This is the last project of my Core 2 Interaction Studio, I decided to make something cute and fun with my favorite video game character, Star Kirby. It can be the playing cards for children with some simple words at the bottom-left.

To experiment with the input and output function in Javascript, I tried something simple like Keyboard interaction, keyup and keydown functions. The users can freely press any keys to change the kirby and background color.
